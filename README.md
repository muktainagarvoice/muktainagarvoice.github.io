## About

This repository documents the ongoing land rights issue related to Plot No. 73 situated in Punarvasan Tappa 2, Muktainagar, Jalgaon, Maharashtra.

The owner is a 70-year-old retired employee of the Ministry of Defence who spent his career serving India's defence manufacturing sector.

During his retirement years, he is seeking official measurement (Mojni), proper boundary determination, and restoration of the original area of his allotted plot.

This repository serves as a public archive of documents, RTI applications, government correspondence, evidence, and timelines.

## The Story

Plot No. 73 was originally allotted under Punarvasan Tappa 2.

The original plot measured approximately **185.80 square meters (2,000 square feet).**

Today, only around **500 square feet** appears to remain physically available.

After decades of service in the Ministry of Defence, a senior citizen now faces another battle.

The family seeks:

- Official Mojni (measurement)
- Proper boundary determination
- Verification of land records
- Lawful removal of encroachment if established through official measurement
- Restoration of the original allotted area
